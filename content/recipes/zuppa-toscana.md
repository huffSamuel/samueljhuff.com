---
title: Zuppa Toscana
yield: 8 servings
ingredients:
  - sausage:
        amount: '1 1/2'
        unit: cups
        notes: spicy
  - potatoes:
        amount: '2'
        unit: 'medium'
        processing: cut in half lengthwise, then cut into 1/4" slices
  - onions:
        amount: '3/4'
        unit: cups
        processing: diced
  - bacon:
        amount: '6'
        unit: 'slices'
  - garlic:
        amount: '1 1/2'
        unit: teaspoons
        processing: minced
  - kale leaves:
        amount: '2'
        unit: cups
        processing: massaged and cut into bite size pieces
  - chicken base:
        amount: '2'
        unit: tablespoons
  - water:
        amount: '1'
        unit: quart
  - heavy whipping cream:
        amount: '1/3'
        unit: cup
steps:
  - 'Preheat oven to 300 degrees. Place sausage links onto a sheet pan and bake for 25 minutes, or until done; cut in half length-wise, then cut at an angle into 1/2 inch slices.'
  - 'Place onions and bacon in a large saucepan and cook over medium heat until onions are almost clear. Remove bacon and crumble.'
  - 'Add garlic to the onions and cook an additional 1-minute.'
  - 'Add chicken base, water, and potatoes, simmer 15 minutes.'
  - 'Add crumbled bacon, sausage, kale and cream. Simmer 4 minutes and serve.'
---
