---
title: Cornbread
summary: A delicious cornbread
yield: 6 servings
oven_temp:
    amount: 400
    unit: F
ingredients:
    - flour:
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
    - cornmeal:
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
    - sugar:
        amount: 2/3
        unit: cup
    - salt:
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
    - baking powder:
        amount: 3
        unit: teaspoon
    - egg:
        amount: 1
        unit: teaspoon
    - milk:
        amount: 1
        unit: cup
    - oil:
        amount: 1/3
        unit: cup
steps:
    - Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
    - Mix all wet ingredients in another bowl; then pour into dry ingredients and stir until fully combined and no lumps remain.
    - Pour into an ungreased 8x8 pan or pie plate and bake at 400° for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

---
